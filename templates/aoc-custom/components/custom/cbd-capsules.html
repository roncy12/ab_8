CBD TINCTURES VS. CBD CAPSULES

CBD tinctures and CBD oil capsules are both great for a daily CBD routine, delivering Superior Broad Spectrum CBD formula with few extra ingredients. So why choose one over the other?

CBD tinctures can be faster-acting if you take the CBD oil under your tongue and absorb it through the membrane. With that method you might feel the effects faster, compared to a slower release with ingested CBD.

However, to do that, you need to hold the oil in your mouth for up to 60 seconds before swallowing, so a CBD oil pill is usually faster to actually take - simply swallow the capsule with a sip of water. Moreover, while you have to follow the quantity of tincture that you take carefully, each CBD capsule and softgel has a precisely measured amount of CBD in it, so you can easily keep track of how much you're taking.

CBD tablets also don't have the spill risk of oils, so they're suitable for taking with you on the go.
