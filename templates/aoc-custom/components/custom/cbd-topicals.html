Frequently Asked Questions

CBD topicals consist of creams, lotions, gels, salves, and sprays that are applied directly to the skin – always remember, topicals are not intended for oral consumption.

Best for use with topical support, recovery, and discomfort. Our gels, creams, and lotions contain active ingredients such as menthol, histamine dihydrochloride, or lidocaine that aid in pain recovery. In addition, ingredients such as coconut oil, shea butter, essential oils, and other botanicals are added to benefit everyday topical needs.

Gently wash and dry the affected area then apply a generous amount of cream, gel, or lotion onto the affected area and massage into the skin until fully absorbed. For a topical roller, clean the affected area and apply the applicator tip in a dabbing motion directly to the skin. Use more pressure against the skin to increase the fluid application or use less pressure to reduce the amount of fluid applied. For topical spray, clean and dry the affected area then spray an ample amount and let dry till absorbed.

The lasting effects of CBD topical creams, lotions, rollers, salves, and sprays will vary from person to person and can greatly depend upon the size of the affected area and the level of discomfort. Please use accordingly for optimal results and maintain daily use for at least 30 days to effectively assess your personal experience.

There is no one-size-fits-all when applying topical CBD. We recommend starting with an ample amount. Slowly increase the amount with each use until the desired effect is achieved. Maintain a daily regimen of application for at least 30 days to effectively assess individual results.
